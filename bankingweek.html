AFRO-STUDENTS BANKING WEEK

An annual event that is aimed at facilitating financial inclusion by creating awareness on importance of banking- savings and investment. The event will highlight various banking products and services designed for students in primary and secondary schools across Africa.

Activities carried out by schools and their students include:

  • Visits to Banks and Central Banks.
  • Workshops, seminars and keynotes on banking.
  • Campaigns/ a walk for banking.
